GOD OF WAR

God of War is a hack and slash video game franchise created by SCE Santa Monica Studio and distributed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It is based on the adventures of a Spartan demigod, Kratos, who faces various characters from Greek and Nordic mythology, both heroes (Heracles, Theseus, Perseus, etc.); mythological species (gorgons, harpies, or minotaurs);

Greek gods (Ares, Poseidon, Zeus, among others), titans (like Cronos) and primordial gods (like Gaia). Although the Spartan warrior is at enmity with most of the gods, he receives help from many of them at some point in each installment, especially Athena.
